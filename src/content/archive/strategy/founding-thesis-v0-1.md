## The hunch

Small venues — breweries, sneaker shops, climbing gyms — sit on real communities
but have no tooling to activate them. Social platforms rent them reach; nothing
lets them *own* the room.

## The bet (v0.1 wording)

If we give a venue a lightweight, branded way to know and reach its regulars,
the venue will pay because the alternative is an algorithm they do not control.

## What we do not know yet

- Whether venues feel this pain sharply enough to pay.
- Whether "regulars" is even a list the venue can name.
- What the wedge feature is.
