## The thesis, locked

Rebl turns a venue's dormant community into recurring attendance. We prove it
services-first: three **founding houses**, one owned night each, run with us for
90 days at founding terms. Tooling is extracted from what the services work proves.

## Why services-first

- Evidence beats roadmap: every night run is an interview we get paid to conduct.
- The tool we would have built in isolation was wrong twice already (see v0.1, v0.2).
- Three houses is the most one founder can service without hiring.

## The founding-house offer

| Term | Founding house | Later |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Slots | 3 only | open |
| Window | signs within the 90-day window | — |
| Price | founding rate, locked for 12 months | list price |
| We provide | night format, promo kit, run-of-show, list capture | tooling |

## Kill criteria

If by the target date we cannot fill one night repeatably at any of the three
houses, the thesis is wrong in its current form. Write the post-mortem, do not
negotiate with the deadline.

### What would make us wrong

- Venues take the night but attendance does not repeat without discounts.
- The founder is the product — nights only work when we run them in person.
