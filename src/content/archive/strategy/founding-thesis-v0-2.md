## What changed since v0.1

Three informal venue chats. The pain is real but mislocated: venues do not want a
CRM, they want **fuller rooms on dead nights**. The list is a means, not the product.

## The bet (v0.2 wording)

Venues will pay for a system that reliably turns their existing community into
attendance on the nights they choose — starting with one recurring, venue-branded
night we help them run.

## The wedge

One dead night, made alive, monthly. Everything else (the list, the tooling, the
brand kit) hangs off that recurring proof.

## Still open

- Pricing: flat monthly vs. per-night.
- Whether we run the first nights ourselves (services-first) or tool-first.
