## Weekly review (Sunday, 30 min)

1. Update every pipeline row touched this week; set `updated` dates.
2. Move done tasks, refill `today` and `this_week` in `/admin`.
3. Add any decision made this week to the journal — with the reason.
4. Update metrics: venues contacted, interviews, burn.
5. Skim `now/operating-focus` — still true? If not, revise it.

## Money

- Burn target: under $900/month until first founding house signs.
- Every new subscription gets a decision-journal entry under `money`.
