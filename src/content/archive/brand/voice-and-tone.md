## The voice

Quietly confident. A person, not a platform. We say the specific true thing
instead of the impressive vague thing.

## Rules

- Short sentences. Concrete nouns. No exclamation marks.
- Never "revolutionize", "unlock", "supercharge", "community-driven".
- Numbers over adjectives: "30% return rate" beats "amazing retention".
- We admit what is early or unknown — the caveat is part of the brand.

## Register by surface

### DMs and email

Warm, direct, one ask. Reads like a regular texting the owner, not a vendor.

### Pitch and docs

Plain declarative. Tables over paragraphs where terms are involved.

### This site (Rebl HQ)

Instrument-panel terse. Labels, numbers, dates. The design system does the talking.

## Litmus test

Read it aloud in a bar at normal volume. If it sounds embarrassing, rewrite it.
