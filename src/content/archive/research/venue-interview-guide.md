## Ground rules

- 20 minutes, in their venue if possible, on their slowest night if possible.
- We are not pitching. If they ask what we sell, answer honestly and return to questions.
- Raw notes land in this section within 24 hours, one doc per interview.

## The script

### Their room today

1. Walk me through a great night here. What made it work?
2. Which night hurts most? What have you tried on it?
3. Who are your regulars? Could you reach 20 of them tonight if you needed to?

### Money

4. What does a dead {weeknight} cost you, roughly?
5. What do you spend on promotion now, and on what?

### The tell

6. If someone reliably filled that night twice a month, what would that be worth?
   *(Listen for a number. "Exposure" or vagueness = cold. A number = warm.)*

## Scoring

After each interview, mark the venue's warmth in the pipeline:

- **cold** — polite, no number, no pain articulated
- **warm** — named the pain, gave a number or asked about price
- **regular** — asked "when can we start" energy

## Anti-patterns

Do not average interviews into a blended narrative. Contradictions between venues
are data — log them per doc and let the thesis versioning absorb the changes.
